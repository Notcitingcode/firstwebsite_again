WELCOME TO AJEZH.NET!

March 7th, 2025

That’s right! I, Ajezh (Uh-jeesh) have created a website! Whether you know me as a friend, acquaintance, teacher, or mortal enemy. Eventually, you’ll see this very page, because I’ll show you! Now, I’ve already ended ¾ of these sentences with exclamation marks, so let’s get into the actual stuff. The what, and the why.

The History behind this place

So, Ajezh.net started as a passion project that I have actually been doing for a little while. It started when my dad first introduced me to HTML5 - the first coding language I ever learned. One day, he said I should make a website. So, I did! We called it Ajezh.net… that was 5 years ago… so much has changed since then... i was the valedictorian. i became a sophisticated individual. i learned so much more when it came to the world of coding. But, this website always came back to me. Something I should do, eventually. Every year, on my birthday, I create a webpage stating my coding acknowledgments, and what I wanted to do in the future. 2024 wasn’t a big year for coding, but I still wanted to put something on the website. You know what came next…

Okay, but what actually is this?

Ajezh.net is just a place where I can post a bunch of goofy nonsense. Some of the nonsense that will be here is cool, like video games, and my blog. Other stuff here is literal nonsense. Such as my Book of Nonsense. Some of the stuff here will be polished, other stuff will literally be written in the default Times New Roman, size 12, white background, default of doom. One thing is for certain however, none of the stuff here will be consistent. What does that mean? Well, scroll up, back to the heading. Now look behind the big Ajezh.net sign. It's pixel art. Now, look at the rest of the page… gradients. A lot of the pages on this website will be in different styles. I actually really like this idea, because it not only gives me room to grow as a developer. But, it also works as a gimmick for this place...
